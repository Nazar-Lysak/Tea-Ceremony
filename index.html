About The Company

We want to share the beautiful tea culture with the world.

Get rid of fatigue and discover new tea varieties. The tea ceremony clears your mind, helps concentrate and fills your body with healthy elements.

The tea degustation is conducted unhurriedly, so take your time to forget about the bustling city and fully enjoy the drink which was discovered more than 2000 years ago.

In addition to simply serving and drinking tea, one of the core goals of this ceremony is to allow guests to enjoy the host’s hospitality in an atmosphere different from the common everyday life.

Tea Heritage

Tea is an ancient drink that perfectly quenches thirst, helps to concentrate and clear the mind. Initially, tea culture originated in China, but now there are a huge number of amazing varieties from Japan, Ceylon, Turkey and other regions. We have a unique collection of tea varieties from around the world and are always happy to tell you about it, share the unique taste of each drink with you.

Tea degustation is a whole ritual, which is held at an easy pace, with pleasant relaxing music, in a darkened room. Our tea master will tell you about each type of tea in detail, teach you how to brew it and introduce you to the tea culture.

China, Japan and More

In general, tea culture can be divided into two branches, traditional and modern. We usually think of Chinese, Japanese and Turkish teas when speaking about the traditional tea culture. All these varieties of tea appeared many years ago and passed the test of time to be in our collection today.

Among the more modern teas, we offer you to taste unique varieties collected in Ceylon and the island of Java. Definitely, these teas can compete with traditional Japanese and Chinese drinks.

Matcha Tea Degustation

Matcha tea is a new trend with a long history. In fact, this type of tea was discovered in the days of the Chinese Song dynasty. It has a lot of health benefits and is commonly used to lose weight, boost energy and detox. Matcha tasting is included in our tea ceremony.
